About Borderless Tech Academy

Building Africa's Future, One Developer at a Time

Our Vision

To make Africans globally employable by providing practical, borderless tech education that bridges classroom learning and real-world experience.

Our Mission

Empower 10,000 Africans by 2030 with in-demand global tech skills and mentorship from industry experts, enabling them to earn in dollars from anywhere in the world.

WHY BTA

There are several tech training institutes all across Africa, but what makes us standout is the real life tech experience we provide to all our students. We don't just teach theory, we provide practical opportunities for every learner to utilize the skills they learnt in class on a real life project. Thus, you either continue on the project and turn it to a real life start up or you are equipped with practical scenarios to describe in an interview session.

Our Manifesto

We are dreamers. Builders. Believers in a new Africa — one where talent, not geography, defines destiny.

At Borderless Tech Academy (BTA), we believe hope is not a privilege; it's a skill. A skill that can be learned, built, and deployed — just like DevOps, Cloud, or Code.

We see a generation of Africans tired of waiting for opportunities to come. So we teach them to create their own.

With a laptop, an internet connection, and the right mindset, anyone can rise — from Lagos to London, Nairobi to New York, Accra to Amsterdam.

We don't just teach technology — we teach freedom.

Freedom from limits. Freedom from borders. Freedom to dream, build, and belong to a global digital tribe.
